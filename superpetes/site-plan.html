Overview

Purpose

Family is everything, right? But then we grow up. We move out. Many move on. As for me, I would rather not. I love my family . They are everything to me, so I'm making a website to keep us connected. It will be exclusively our page. Keeping up with all the future, nieces, nephews, grandkids, cousins, brothers, sisters, aunts, uncles, and grandparents will be even easier that opening up Facebook. Why? It will just be us.

Audience

My direct family members (parents, siblings, future children, etc.)
